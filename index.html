Description

What this application basically does is that it take the information that you have inputed,does some calculation and finds the day of the week that your were born and it gives an output of your Akan name.What do i mean when i say an Akan name?Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture, Frequently in Ghana, children are given their first name as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born.
